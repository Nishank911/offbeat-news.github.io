Hello. We are offbeat.



Our mission
Ignite intellect & innovation through information centricity.

With all the negativity filled around us, we bring forth you a whiff of fresh air, aligned with your interests. Thus, let you discover optimistic and cheerful stories along the way.

Our story

For years, when reading news from different platforms, we realised just how difficult it is to go through them without being assaulted with notifications of a new disaster, conflict, and other upsetting events. Which in-turn has an impact on our thinking, behavior, and emotions.

When much of the media is full of doom and gloom, we are dedicated to deliver quality news about what’s going right, helping you to see the big picture. We believe that positive stories can help people build better empathy for others which is essential for a more meaningfull and fulfilled society.

Who we are for

For the small and big explorer, reader, reporter and optimist in you.
